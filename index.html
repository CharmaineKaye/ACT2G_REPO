About My Cafe Coffee

Delicious! Actually, the way coffee tasters describe their cup of coffee is in terms of aroma, acidity, and body. Aroma is how it smells, whether it be earthy, spicy, floral, or nutty. You probably know that our sense of taste is highly dependant on our sense of smell. Acidity is not talking about the actual pH, it's the bright, tangy, fruity, or wine-like flavor characteristics. Acidity is reduced as the coffee is roasted darker. Body is the physical mouthfeel and texture of the coffee in your mouth, such as light, medium, or full.
